ABOUT US

FAQs

What are NFTs?

NFTs or non-fungible tokens are cryptographic assets on blockchain with unique identification codes and metadata that distinguish them from each other.

What makes the medicosis nft valuable?

The value of an NFT comes from the property it symbolizes, and each Medicosis NFT represents a part of the first ever value driven NFT based medical project, which is aimed at catering to the most fundamental human needs, health, and well-being.

How to validate the authenticity of an nft?

NFT ownership is recorded on the blockchain, and that entry acts as a digital pink slip.

What is does it mean to mint?

Minting a Medicosis means you are purchasing an NFT and making it a verifiable part of the Ethereum blockchain. Your NFT is represented as digital art and can be traded on the market and tracked digitally when it is resold or collected again in the future.

What is a whitelist and how can I join it?

A whitelist is a list of IDs that get minting privileges on an NFT project. People on the whitelist get to mint first at the least price. To join our whitelist, you must first join our discord and engage actively with the community. People with the most engagements and invites will be added to the list and will win giveaways.

Where can I view my nfts?

Once minted, your NFTs will be visible in your metamask or any other wallet used to purchase and you will be able to view, trade, or showcase your NFT.
